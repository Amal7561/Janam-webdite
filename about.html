About Janam

Janam means "people" in Tamil and Malayalam and translates to "birth" in Hindi. The CSR initiative of LegalEase Solutions is named Janam. We believe in the responsibility of contributing back to the societies we are a part of as a habit and not an occasion. We are working to make an impact at the policy level to amplify and sustain change at the grassroots level.
