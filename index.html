Janam – The Story Behind the Name

Janam is a word with different meanings in Tamil, Malayalam, and Hindi. Janam in Tamil and Malayalam means “People” and in Hindi means “Birth”. This name was finalized through a poll among the LegalEasians. Janam is for the people who look upon the law as their last resort and it is a birth of new beginnings for people and organizations requiring legal assistance.
